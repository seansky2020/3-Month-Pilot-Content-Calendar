Weekly Content Cadence (40-50 posts/week)

  • LinkedIn: 4-5 posts/week (long-form articles, carousels, video, text posts)

💡 Implementation Notes

  • Recurring Series: "Wins Wednesday" case study carousel (weekly), LinkedIn Newsletter (monthly), Team Spotlight (bi-weekly)
  • Paid Ads Strategy: Allocate $600 (Oct), $800 (Nov), $800 (Dec) for LinkedIn lead gen ads and Instagram carousel/video ads
  • Community Engagement: Respond to all comments within 24 hours; 30 min/day engagement on industry posts
  • Hashtag Strategy: Use #SouthAfricanBrandStrategy, #AfricanCreativeExcellence, #MadeInJburg, #1983MediaCase consistently
  • Repurposing: Turn LinkedIn articles into 5-part Instagram carousels; convert YouTube videos into 8-10 Instagram Reels
  • Tracking: UTM parameters on all links; Google Analytics 4 + native platform analytics; weekly performance reviews
  • Flexibility: Reserve 10-15% of calendar for real-time news, trends, or client opportunities
  • Resource Needs: Social media manager (1 FTE), content creator (0.5 FTE), video producer (0.5 FTE) minimum

✅ Content Production Workflow

Week 1: Planning

  • Strategy meeting & theme selection
  • Review previous month's analytics
  • Content calendar planning (4-5 weeks)
  • Assign content to team members

Week 2: Creation

  • Copywriting & caption drafts
  • Graphic design & visual creation
  • Video shooting & editing
  • First round of content review

Week 3: Scheduling

  • Final approval & sign-off
  • Load content into scheduler
  • Add hashtags, UTMs, CTAs
  • QA check all posts

Ongoing: Engagement

  • Daily community management (30 min)
  • Respond to comments within 24 hours
  • Monitor mentions & DMs
  • Weekly analytics review
